How do I place an order online?

Ordering online is easy! Simply navigate to our menu, choose your favorite items, and follow the steps at checkout. If you need assistance, our user-friendly interface will guide you through the process.

Are there gluten-free or vegan options available?

Yes, we offer a variety of gluten-free and vegan options. Check our menu or contact us for specific dietary inquiries. We believe everyone should enjoy our delicious treats!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards and online payment gateways. Your transactions are secure, and we strive to provide convenient options for our customers.

Can I customize my order for special occasions?

Absolutely! We love adding a personal touch to your celebrations. Contact us in advance, and we'll work together to create a custom order tailored to your special event.

What is your delivery area, and is there a minimum order requirement?

We deliver in Colombo destrict. Check our delivery page for more details. While there may be a minimum order for delivery, it varies based on location. Details are provided during the checkout process.
